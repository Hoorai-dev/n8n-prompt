- * `extra`: **Questions**. Title: "💡 還可以問：". `extra_text`: 2 "What if" or "Why" questions to deepen the strategy or empathy.(separated by `\n`).



**Card 4: 🎮 親子遊戲 (3 mins)**
* **Goal:** **Verbal Gamification (Conversation-based Play)**.
* **Constraint (CRITICAL):** **NO PROPS ALLOWED.** Must be playable purely through talking (e.g., guessing, verbal challenges, reaction games).

* `p2`: **How to Play**. Title: "🎬 怎麼玩：". Text: Simple steps to play using words only.
* `extra`: **Swap**. Title: "🔄玩法延伸 ：". Text: 2 ways to change the rules or roles to keep it fresh.


**Card 5: ✨ 儀式收尾 (1 min)**
